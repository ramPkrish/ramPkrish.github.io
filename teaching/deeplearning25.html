CDS301

Deep Learning

Instructor

Dr Ram Prasad K, PhD

Associate Professor, Sai University, Chennai



Overview

Deep Learning is one of the fast developing approaches within Machine Learning which learns hierarchical feature representations of the data. This course will comprise both theoretical framework and hands-on experience for artificial neural network and deep learning architectures for a variety of data modalities such as structured (tabular) and unstructured (text and image) data.


Course Outline

  • Basics of artificial neural network, Regression, Classification
  • Linear models for regression and classification, Perceptrons, Multi-Layer Perceptrons.
  • Deep Neural Networks, Data Normalization, Types of Gradient Descent.
  • Training DNN, Backpropagation, Chain rule and Numerical Differentiation
  • Vanishing/Exploding gradient problems; Optimizers
  • Overfitting and Underfitting; Regularization - L1, L2 and Dropout
  • Fully connected networks for regression and classification
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for Computer Vision and Transfer Learning
  • Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) for sequential data (Time series, Natural Language Processing)
  • Longs Short Term Memory (LSTM) and Gated Recurrent Units (GRU)
  • Attention mechanisms and Transformers
