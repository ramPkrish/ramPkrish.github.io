Dr Ram Prasad Krishnamoorthy

Associate Professor & Associate Dean
School of Computing and Data Science
Sai University, Chennai, India

My research interest focus on the intersection between computer vision and machine learning. I received my Ph.D degree in Computer Science Engineering with distinction "Sobresaliente Cum Laude" from Universidad Aut√≥noma de Madrid, Spain, and pursued my postdoctoral research work at Dublin City University, Ireland. My doctoral and postdoctoral research works were funded through the prestigious European Union Marie Curie Fellowship. My PhD work got nominated for the prestigious European Biometrics Research and Industry Award. I held visiting researcher position at University of Twente, Netherlands and University of Halmstad, Sweden. I graduated in Computer Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. My expertise is in the areas of classical machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, biometrics, software developments and algorithm design. I was also the founder and director of VisionCog Research and Development Private Limited, India and manages commercial R&D works in the domain of computer vision and biometrics. Currently, I am the Associate Dean for School of Computing and Data Science at Sai University. I also lead the Biometrics and Pattern Analysis Research Group.
